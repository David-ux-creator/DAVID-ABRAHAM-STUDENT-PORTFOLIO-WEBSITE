01 A Beginning, Logged

I'm David Abraham, a first-year student of the BSc Cybersecurity programme at Miva Open University, currently based in Ikorodu, Lagos. My academic journey has just started, but my curiosity about how systems are built, broken, and defended started long before I ever wrote my first line of code. I'm here to learn the fundamentals properly: how the web works, how networks talk to each other, and how attackers think — so that one day I can be the person standing between a system and the people trying to break it.

This platform is both my digital introduction and my working academic tool. It holds my story, my earliest projects, and the planner I use to keep my coursework in order. Everything here reflects exactly where I am: Level 100, early in the process, and fully committed to it.

About Me

The background, the goals, and the toolkit I'm building them with.

01 Educational Background

I am currently a 100-Level student pursuing a BSc in Cybersecurity at Miva Open University, one of Nigeria's leading online-first institutions. My first semester is focused on foundational courses that build the mathematical, computing, and communication base every cybersecurity professional needs — including this very course, COS 106: Introduction to Web Technologies, through which this platform was designed and built.

As a 100-level student, I'm at the very start of a five-level programme, and I'm approaching it with the mindset that strong fundamentals now will pay off in every advanced course that follows.

02 Career Aspirations

My long-term goal is to work in digital infrastructure protection — helping organisations defend their networks, systems, and data against evolving threats. I'm particularly drawn to network security and ethical hacking, and I want to eventually earn industry certifications that complement my degree.

  • Understand how networks and systems are architected before learning how to secure them
  • Build strong, practical skills in scripting and automation
  • Contribute to safer digital spaces for individuals and businesses in Nigeria and beyond
